What is Kontent_69?

Kontent_69 helps creators, brands, and marketers automate their TikTok content strategy. Using Make (Integromat) and the official TikTok Content Posting API, users can securely upload, schedule, and auto-publish TikTok videos without manual effort.

Our platform handles authentication, content delivery, and visibility settings — streamlining your publishing workflow and improving efficiency. All interactions are authorized through TikTok's secure OAuth 2.0 flow.
